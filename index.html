HOW TO USE

  • 1. PICK A COLOUR: Drag the marker on the custom chart to set your base colour.
  • 2. DEFINE STEP GAP: Use the slider to set the exact percentage difference between each adjacent shade.
  • 3. CHOOSE MODE: Generate both lighter/darker shades, just tints, or just dark shades.
  • 4. CLICK TO COPY: Tap any individual colour card to copy it, or use the big button at the bottom to copy the entire palette!
